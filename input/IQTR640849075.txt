"MHR Capital Partners Master Account III LP and MHR Institutional Partners IV LP managed by MHR Fund Management LLC agreed to acquire an additional 2.9% stake in Lions Gate Entertainment Corp. (NYSE:LGF.A) from affiliates of John C. Malone for $22.1 million on October 2, 2019. Pursuant to the terms of agreement, MHR will pay $9.19 per share and will acquire 2.39 million shares of Lions Gate.
