"DOF ASA (OB:DOF) entered into a share purchase agreement to acquire 35.1% stake in DOF Subsea AS from Dolphin Invest 2 As and an unknown seller for NOK 20 million on November 18, 2019. DOF ASA will acquire 58.7 million shares. Out of which, 51.1 million shares will be acquired by unknown seller and 7.5 million from Dolphin Invest 2 As. The acquisition is closely associated with the ongoing refinancing of DOF Subsea AS. Post acquisition, DOF ASA will be sole shareholder of DOF Subsea AS.
