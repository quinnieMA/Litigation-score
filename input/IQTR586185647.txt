"An unknown buyer entered into share purchase agreement to acquire 2.7% stake in Largo Resources Ltd. (TSX:LGO) from Arias Resource Capital Fund L.P. managed by Arias Resource Capital Management LP for $28.8 million on October 5, 2018. Pursuant to the agreement, Arias sold 13.86 million shares at a price of $2.08 per share. Following the sale, Arias will own a 2.6% stake or 13.58 million common shares of Largo and its sponsor, Arias Resource Capital Management LP will own 45.9% stake in Largo through its funds including Arias.
