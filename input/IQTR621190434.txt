"High River LP, Icahn Capital LP and Icahn Partners Master Fund LP acquired 6.2% stake in Caesars Entertainment Corporation (NasdaqGS:CZR) from TPG Capital, L.P. and Apollo Management, L.P. for approximately $330 million on March 7, 2019. Apollo and TPG sold an aggregate of 38.9 million shares of Common Stock at a price of $8.50 per share. As a result of this acquisition, Carl Icahn increased his ownership stake in Caesars Entertainment to 15.53 percent.
